Quesodilla

Descripton

Nice and melty, hits the spot when you want something kinda fatty and unhealthy.

Ingredients

  • 1 large flour tortilla
  • Quarter of a stick of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups shredded chedder cheese

Steps

  1. Put frying pan on medium heat, and start to melt the butter.
  2. Place tortilla in pan, flat.
  3. Flip once after about 20 secs.
  4. Layer cheese on to start to melt.
  5. After cheese is starting to melt, fold over tortilla.
  6. Flip tortilla to prvent burning.
