Sandwich

Descripton

This quick sandwich will satisfy but not break the bank

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 3 slices of deli meat
  • 2 slices of cheese (to pair with meat)
  • Mayonaise
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Place meat and cheese on top of one slice of bread
  2. Add mayonaise and muster to the other slice of bread
  3. Place one slice on top of the other
