Scrambled Eggs

Descripton

This simple recipe is a staple of every American home. Great for doing with kids or just quick and filling

Ingredients

  • 3-4 Large Eggs
  • Quarter of a stick of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded chedder cheese

Steps

  1. Put frying pan on medium heat, and start to melt the butter.
  2. Crack eggs into bowl.
  3. Use a fork to mix vigorusly, adding as much air as possible
  4. When butter is melted but not burnt, add eggs to pan.
  5. Scrape pan with spatula as eggs cook to bunch up egg.
  6. Flip as needed to cook both sides
  7. Add cheese to melt on top when egg looks almost finished.
  8. When all egg is firm but not spongy, place on plate and serve.
